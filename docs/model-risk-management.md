# Model Risk Management

## Purpose

Model risk management identifies how models can fail, drift, or behave in ways that create business risk.

## Risk Categories

- performance risk
- drift risk
- explainability risk
- bias risk
- operational risk

## Operating Questions

- what could go wrong?
- how will it be monitored?
- what is the fallback?
- who approves use?

## Evidence To Collect

- risk assessment
- test results
- monitoring plan
- escalation path
