# Compliance Alignment

## Purpose

Compliance alignment maps AI controls to applicable regulatory, contractual, and internal requirements.

## Alignment Areas

- governance approvals
- data protection
- audit evidence
- model documentation
- monitoring and review
- policy exceptions
- periodic reassessment

## Evidence To Collect

- crosswalk matrix
- approval records
- audit artifacts
- review history

## Alignment Outcome

The aim is to show how the AI governance model satisfies internal policy, external regulation, and practical operating expectations at the same time.
