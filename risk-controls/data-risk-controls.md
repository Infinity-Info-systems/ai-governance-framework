# Data Risk Controls

## Purpose

Data risk controls reduce the likelihood of sensitive, low-quality, or unsuitable data creating AI risk.

## Control Areas

- data classification
- access restrictions
- masking or anonymization
- retention
- lineage and source validation
- privacy review
- data quality checks

## Evidence To Collect

- classification records
- access logs
- masking evidence
- lineage review

## Data Rule

If the data cannot be explained, trusted, and limited appropriately, the AI use case should not move forward.
